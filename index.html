A Little About Me

The winding path that brought me here

I've always been drawn to the spaces between things - between traditional and alternative, between business and healing, between who we are and who we're becoming.

My path into psychedelic work wasn't linear. I came to it through personal experience with healing, a deep belief that mental health care can be so much more than it currently is, and a fascination with consciousness itself.

These days, I spend my time helping Center for Conscious Alchemy grow thoughtfully - finding incredible clinicians, building systems that support both clients and practitioners, and trying to bridge the gap between the psychedelic renaissance and accessible, integrated mental health care.

When I'm not thinking about work, I'm probably exploring Chicago, reading about consciousness, or working on Believr - a little app I co-created to help LGBTQ+ Christians find community and belonging.
